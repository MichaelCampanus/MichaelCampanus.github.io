Online Classes

I teach private lessons online. Classes are conducted in Latin, but are suitable for beginners. Individuals are $40/hour, $25/half-hour, and groups are $25/hour per participant. To inquire about lessons, contact me by email.

Current Offerings

Intro to Latin
We will read and discuss Fr. Most's Latin by the Natural Method, covering all the basics of the Latin language with a focus on oral proficiency and reading comprehension, with the goal of preparing students to read the Vulgate.

Vulgate
An intermediate class reading selections from the Gospels, Psalms, and other narrative books of Jerome's Latin translation of the Bible.

Latin Homilies
A survey of Latin homilies from St. Augustine, St. Gregory the Great, St. Leo the Great, and St. Bernard of Clairvaux.
